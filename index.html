Tollywood Actors

Pawan Kalyan

About:

Konidela Pawan Kalyan (born Konidela Kalyan Babu; 2 September 1971) is an Indian actor, politician, filmmaker, martial artist, and philanthropist who works in Telugu cinema. Known for his unique acting style and mannerisms, he has a large fan base and a cult following.

Achievements:

Winning the National Award and six Nandi Awards for the film "Tholi Prema" in 1998. Being named one of India's Top 5 Heroes in a Star India survey in 2014. Being ranked 24th in the Forbes India list of top 100 Celebrities in 2018.

Venkatesh

About:

Telugu actor Daggubati Venkatesh, popularly known as Venkatesh, enjoys a massive fan following. Owing to the huge commercial success of his films, he has been bestowed with the honorary title of ‘Victory Venkatesh’. He is celebrating his 61st birthday today.

Achievements:

In 1986, Venkatesh debuted with K. Raghavendra Rao directed Kaliyuga Pandavulu for which he won his first Nandi Award that recognises excellence in Telugu cinema, Telugu theatre, and Telugu television and Lifetime achievements in Indian cinema.
